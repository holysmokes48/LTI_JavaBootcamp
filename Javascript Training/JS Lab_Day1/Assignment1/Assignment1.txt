Day 1: Understanding Browsers and HTML

Questions from self study slides:
1. For which tag, Font size is bigger, <H2> or <H5>?
Answer: <H2>

2. How many levels of Heading Tags are there?
Answer: 6 levels

3. If you write <H1> Heading 1 </H1> <H2>Heading 2 </H2>, Heading 1 Heading 2 will be printed at the same time or in different lines?
Answer: Heading 1 will be printed first on at the top of the webpage, and then Heading 2 will be printed on the following line.
